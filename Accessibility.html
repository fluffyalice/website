Visual impairment

- For people who are color blind, the website color matching is alternated between cold and warm colors, and all the color are color-friendly to them.

- For senior citizens or people who are suffering from myopia, the website is designed with large fonts and buttons.

- The main color of the website is designed with dark color, in order to protect people's eyes from bright screen.

Physical impairment

- For people with physical disabilities, each module of the site has a direct distance to aviod clicking incorrectly.

Cognitive impairment

- For people who have trouble concentrating, memorizing, understanding, recognizing and speaking, website will have flexible and simple user screen: minimize the use of buttons, and reduce screen clutter. In addition, the website will alse use plain language and simple font.
