  • Media booths (1)/Media room (2):

    • There are a lot of professional equipment, including camera, speakers, iMac, volume controller, DV deck, AV switcher, and microphone. In addition, there are many special applications which are only set for students who are majoring in media.

  • Library reference collection (3):

    • The collection contains hundreds of books which are the most popular in all departments, and it cannot be borrowed.

  • Library skills room (4):

    • There allow students to discover, understand, question, reference, create, communicate.

  • Silent study room (5):

    • There are many individual study desks in the silent study room, which are equipped with whiteboard, bookshelf, desk lamp and computer, and students will not worry about disturbing others or being disturbed by others, because each study desk is independent and equipped with boards to block the opposite desk. In addition, the high-sided arm chairs with small working surfaces which is suitable for independent work were also found in the silent study room. Students can concentrate on their studies better here.

  • Group study room (6)/Flexible space (7):

    • There are a lot of irregular shaped desk and kite shaped tables on wheels in flexible space and group study room separately, which encourages users to share the space. Seven group study room can contain around fifty-five people.

  • Library and CICS help service (8):

    • If students have any problems in the library, they can ask for help here. They provide stuff face-to-face support, telephone/email support, and equipment support. For example, if students have a problem with printing, connect, forgetting password.
