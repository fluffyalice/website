The exterior of the whole building is made of glass in the shape of dark blue, light blue and white crystals. Each glass is directly connected with aluminum alloy, so it is called the Diamond. The Diamond is designed to provide students with a comfortable and quiet learning environment and wonderful classes. There are thousands of computers and hundreds of activity rooms in the library, including group study room, silent study room, computer room, media room and so on. The Diamond has four floors, and this website will give you a detailed description of the fourth floor.
